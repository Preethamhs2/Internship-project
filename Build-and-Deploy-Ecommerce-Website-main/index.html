Can i cancel my order?

Orders are processed and sent to our fulfillment center within 20 minutes of the order being completed. We can cancel an order at no charge if we are notified within 15 minutes of your order being placed. Please call us at (385) 219-4767 between 8am-5pm Pacific Standard Time for immediate assistance. Emails will not guarantee order cancellations, as they are answered in the order they are received. Orders cannot be cancelled after this 15 minute window. Preorders and special orders cannot be cancelled as the funds have already been sent to the manufacturer to secure your watch.

Can i apply more than one coupon code per order?

e-commerce platform only allows one coupon code to be applied to an order. Coupon codes cannot be combined with any cart-level discounts applied to your order.
